How to Use the Image Classifier

  1. Upload an image you want to classify.
  2. Wait for the classifier to process the image.
  3. View the top 5 predictions made by the classifier.
  4. Each prediction will display the label and probability of the object in the image.

Instructions:

  1. Click the "Choose File" button.
  2. Select an image from your device.
  3. Click "Classify Food Image".
  4. Wait for the classifier to analyze the image.
  5. View the top 5 predictions displayed below the image.
  6. Each prediction will show the label and probability of the object in the image.
